Motivation

The work I do is fueled by the things that motivate me, such as:

  • Security - nothing is 100% secure, but that doesn't mean you can settle for 70%
  • Accessibility - design and implementation should be as inclusive as possible
  • Minimalism - let the core experience speak for itself; minimize the bells and whistles
  • Extensibility - design your product with the expectation that someone will try to modify it to make it work better for them
  • Happiness - do something that people will appreciate. I personally find happiness in creating or doing something that brings people joy
